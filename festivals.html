Major Festivals
ముఖ్యమైన పండుగలు

Checklists for the most celebrated annual festivals in our tradition.

Vinayaka Chavithi (వినాయక చవితి)

The grand celebration for Lord Ganesha, including the unique 21-leaf 'Patri' puja.

Dasara Navaratri (దసరా నవరాత్రి)

A 10-day festival honoring Goddess Durga, with daily alankarams and naivedyams.

Varalakshmi Vratam (వరలక్ష్మీ వ్రతం)

An important puja for married women to seek the blessings of Goddess Varalakshmi.

Sri Rama Navami (శ్రీ రామనవమి)

Celebrating the birth of Lord Rama with specific offerings like Panakam and Vadapappu.
