Life Ceremonies
సంస్కారాలు

Checklists for the sacred rites of passage that mark the major milestones in life.

Gruhapravesam (గృహప్రవేశం)

The housewarming ceremony to purify and sanctify a new home before moving in.

Namakaranam (నామకరణం)

The traditional naming ceremony for a newborn child, usually held on the 11th or 21st day.

Upanayanam (ఉపనయనం)

The sacred thread ceremony, a significant rite of passage for young boys into student life.

Vivaham (వివాహం)

The wedding ceremony, with detailed lists for both the bride's and groom's sides.

Seemantham (సీమంతం)

A ceremony performed during pregnancy to bless the mother-to-be and the unborn child.

Shashtipoorthi (షష్టి పూర్తి)

A grand celebration marking the completion of 60 years, renewing vows and praying for longevity.
