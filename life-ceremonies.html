Life Ceremonies
సంస్కారాలు

Checklists for the sacred rites of passage that mark the major milestones in life.

Gruhapravesam (గృహప్రవేశం)

The housewarming ceremony to purify and sanctify a new home before moving in.

Namakaranam (నామకరణం)

The traditional naming ceremony for a newborn child.

Annaprasana (అన్నప్రాశన)

The "first rice" ceremony celebrating a baby's first intake of solid food.

Aksharabhyasam (అక్షరాభ్యాసం)

The ceremony to initiate a child into the world of education and letters.

Upanayanam (ఉపనయనం)

The sacred thread ceremony, a significant rite of passage for young boys.

Nischitartham (నిశ్చయ తాంబూలాలు)

The formal engagement ceremony where the alliance between two families is fixed.

Vivaham (వివాహం)

The wedding ceremony, with detailed lists for both the bride's and groom's sides.

Seemantham (సీమంతం)

A ceremony performed during pregnancy to bless the mother-to-be and the unborn child.

Shashtipoorthi (షష్టి పూర్తి)

A grand celebration marking the completion of 60 years of life.
