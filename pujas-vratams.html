Specific Pujas & Vratams
ప్రత్యేక పూజలు & వ్రతాలు

Checklists for special pujas and vratams performed for specific needs, wishes, or occasions.

Satyanarayana Swamy Vratam (సత్యనారాయణ స్వామి వ్రతం)

A popular puja for general well-being, prosperity, and expressing gratitude to Lord Vishnu.

Chandi Homam (చండీ హోమం)

A powerful fire ritual dedicated to Goddess Chandi to overcome obstacles and for protection.

Abhishekam (అభిషేకం)

A general checklist for the sacred bathing ritual performed for a deity's idol.

Vahana Puja (వాహన పూజ)

A simple yet important puja performed when purchasing a new vehicle.

Shankusthapana (శంఖుస్థాపన)

The foundation stone laying ceremony (Bhoomi Puja) for a new building.

Prarambhotsavam (ప్రారంభోత్సవం)

The inauguration ceremony for a new office, shop, or business.

Devata Kalyanam (దేవతా కళ్యాణం)

The Celestial Wedding ceremony performed for deities, filled with joyous rituals.
